StringSense

An interactive tool that helps you memorize the fretboard of your guitar in a fun and engaging way. Level up your knowledge today with StringSense's unique exercises and challenges!

Practice With Your Guitar in Hand

StringSense is designed to be used alongside your instrument. Connect what you see on screen to what you feel in your hands. You'll develop long-term muscle memory that you can rely on when playing, improvising, or writing music.

Diverse Exercises

  • Customizable settings to focus on specific areas and adjust your challenge level
  • Drills for note identification across all strings and positions
  • Randomized patterns to keep you on your toes
  • Expansive modes to keep things fresh

Track Your Progress

  • View statistics on your accuracy and response time
  • Monitor your improvement over time
  • Set goals and unlock achievements to keep you motivated
  • Compete with friends and other players on the leaderboard
